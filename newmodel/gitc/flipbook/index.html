Sketchfab Offline

You’ve asked for it, we’ve delivered.
Now you can share your work wherever you are.
Even without an internet connection.

Sketchfab Offline™ allows you to generate a Sketchfab Flipbook™,
the ultimate offline experience.

How to assemble your Sketchfab Flipbook

Print

Use your regular color printer to print the generated document.
Rigid paper usually gives better results.

Cut

Follow the lines to cut the pages with cisors then stack them in order.

Assemble

Add a clip or staple to hold the pages together.
